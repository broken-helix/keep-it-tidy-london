Why Litter Pick?

Environment

A million pieces of litter are dropped in the UK every day. By picking up the litter, you are helping the environment and making your home look and feel better

Health

Litter picking is great for your health. It gets you outside walking, reaching and carrying and gives you a reason to move.

Community

When people see you picking up litter, they talk to you. By attending organised events, you can meet up with other like-minded people and make new connections.

Mind

Litter picking has been shown to improve mental well-being. As well as providing exercise, you get a great sense of satisfaction and empowerment when you see the impact.
